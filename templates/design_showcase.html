Voice & Tone

✓ Do This

  • "Take your time exploring"

    Encouraging, no pressure

  • "Here's what I've learned"

    Sharing experience, not lecturing

  • "Let's work through this together"

    Collaborative, supportive

✗ Avoid This

  • "Click here now!"

    Too pushy, marketing-speak

  • "You must do this"

    Commanding, not guiding

  • "Limited time offer"

    Creates false urgency
